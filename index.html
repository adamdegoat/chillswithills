Field Tip

Bin the stretchy bungee guy-lines that came with your awning and re-rig with 5mm Dyneema, then carry two 230mm (9 in) screw-in auger anchors per side. Set them at a 45-degree angle, not straight down.

Elastic cord lets your shelter oscillate in a gust, and oscillation is what folds awning arms — low-stretch line keeps the anchor working from the first second the load arrives. A 45-degree pull fights both the sideways wind and the lift at once, which a vertical peg can't do on hard ground.
